🌌 Space Knowledge Hub

🪨 Asteroids

Rocky objects orbiting the Sun, mostly found in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter.

  • • Size: 1m to 1000km diameter
  • • Composition: Rock, metal, carbon
  • • Famous: Ceres, Vesta, Apophis

☄️ Comets

Icy bodies that develop tails when approaching the Sun, originating from outer solar system.

  • • Composition: Ice, dust, rock
  • • Orbital period: 3-200+ years
  • • Famous: Halley, Hale-Bopp

🌠 Meteors

Small particles burning up in Earth's atmosphere, creating bright streaks of light.

  • • Size: Dust grain to boulder
  • • Speed: 11-72 km/s
  • • Showers: Perseids, Leonids

💥 Impact Events

When space objects collide with Earth, causing craters and environmental effects.

  • • Tunguska: 1908, 2000 km² flattened
  • • Chelyabinsk: 2013, 1500 injured
  • • Chicxulub: 66M years ago, dinosaurs

🛡️ Planetary Defense

Scientists track potentially hazardous objects and develop deflection methods like NASA's DART mission, which successfully altered asteroid Dimorphos's orbit in 2022.
